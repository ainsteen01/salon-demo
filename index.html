About Us

At Trim & Trend, we don’t just cut hair—we craft experiences. Born from a passion for futuristic design and flawless grooming, our salon blends cutting-edge techniques with a sleek, neon-lit atmosphere that turns every visit into a cinematic moment.

Whether you're chasing a bold transformation or a subtle refresh, our stylists are trained in the latest trends and timeless classics. From precision trims to radiant color work, we tailor every service to your unique vibe.
